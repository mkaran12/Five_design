Mission 2022-2027

To Awaken and Equip the Church;
To prepare God's people for Transformative Revival Where we see the Spiritual Atmosphere of the Nation Changed and One Million Souls brought to Christ.

Rhema Missionary Outreach International (RMOI) is a Christian organization dedicated to evangelism, humanitarian aid, and community development on an international scale. Through preaching, teaching, and various forms of outreach, they spread the Christian gospel while also providing essential assistance to communities in need, including food aid, medical care, education, and disaster relief. RMOI aims to empower communities by establishing infrastructure projects like schools, healthcare facilities, and clean water systems, while also offering missionary training to individuals interested in spreading their mission. With a focus on international outreach, RMOI endeavors to make a positive impact in diverse regions worldwide, contributing to the establishment and support of Christian communities and the betterment of society.

Alter of Witness Chapel

Altar of Witness Chapel (AWC) is a Christian church located in Nyeri, Kenya. The church's focus is on loving God and loving people. They believe in serving their community and spreading the message of God's love and grace to all. AWC provides a welcoming and inclusive environment for people of all backgrounds and walks of life to worship and grow in their faith. The church offers various programs and ministries for children, youth, and adults to help them connect with God and each other. At AWC, believers are encouraged to live out their faith and make a positive impact on the world around them
