Alter of Witness Chapel

Altar of Witness Chapel (AWC) is a Christian church located in Nyeri, Kenya. The church's focus is on loving God and loving people. They believe in serving their community and spreading the message of God's love and grace to all. AWC provides a welcoming and inclusive environment for people of all backgrounds and walks of life to worship and grow in their faith. The church offers various programs and ministries for children, youth, and adults to help them connect with God and each other. At AWC, believers are encouraged to live out their faith and make a positive impact on the world around them
